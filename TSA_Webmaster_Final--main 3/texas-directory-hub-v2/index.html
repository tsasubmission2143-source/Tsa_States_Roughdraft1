Connecting Texans to Community Resources

Finding reliable local help can be overwhelming. Our Community Resource Hub brings together food assistance, housing help, health services, education programs, and volunteer opportunities across Texas in one easy-to-use place. We help you discover and access the support you need.

The Problem

Many people struggle to find reliable local resources when they need help. Information is scattered across government sites, nonprofits, and word of mouth—making it hard to know where to turn for food assistance, housing, health care, education, or ways to give back through volunteering.

Our Solution

This website solves that problem by organizing trusted Texas resources in one place. You can search by category, city, or ZIP code and view results on an interactive map. Whether you need help or want to contribute to your community, the hub makes it easier to connect with the right organizations.

About This Hub

Texas Directory Hub is a Community Resource Hub created to help Texans find and access local support services quickly and easily. Our goal is to connect people with food assistance, housing help, health services, education programs, and volunteer opportunities across the state.

We believe everyone deserves to know where to turn when they need help—or when they want to give back. This site brings together trusted government agencies, nonprofits, and community organizations in one place, with search and map tools to make finding resources simple.

How to Use This Site

  • Home — Read about the problem we solve and browse resource categories.
  • Resources — Search and filter by category, city, or ZIP code, and view results on the map.
  • Submit Resource — Suggest a new organization to add to the hub via email.
  • References — View project documents and development tools used to build this site.
